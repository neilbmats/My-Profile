Job Discription

This position at google works closely with security involving all its users around the world. This position requires you to be passionate about safety and should be able to fix, maintain and improve security systems at google which provides a safer operational environment for its users. Protecting highly sensitive information like passwords and customer data while maintaining network equipment and monitoring for threats and attacks is one of the main role of a security engineering manager. Strong communication and leadership skills along with proper time management are another important skills that are essential for this role.
